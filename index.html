Digital Innovation UZH

Website for the former UZH course Digital Innovation.

Syllabus Course Materials Teaching Staff License

To increase accessibility, we have decided to release the teaching materials for the UZH course "Digital Innovation" online. The course is primarily offered to master's students in informatics and business. Please feel free to reach out to us with any feedback or questions you may have.
